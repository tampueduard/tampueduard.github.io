*Nobody else can give you an education* is a Master's thesis by Ilona Pakarinen (Aalto University, School of Arts, Design and Architecture, 2025), exploring self-efficacy and creative identity in 3D animation through autoethnography. The work centres on two short animations that use reflective self-observation as both method and subject.

I contributed the music, sound design, and voice design for the animations. The brief was clear from the start: the audio shouldn't compete with the visuals or the characters, but sit underneath them — supporting the comedic, slightly absurd tone of the piece without drawing attention to itself.

The sound design does most of the work in building the world. Ambient textures, light flickers, and environmental detail give each scene a subtle presence, adding to what's happening without explaining it. The music follows the same logic — sparse and sporadic rather than continuous, appearing in small cues that nudge the mood rather than lead it. The ending is the exception, where the music becomes openly cartoonish, giving the whole thing a lightness that lands the final joke.

The voice design grew out of a similar approach. Ilona had a specific character in mind for the human voice — goofy, a little off. We recorded her voice and I shaped it through processing until it matched that feeling. For the demonic counterpart, I voiced the character myself and applied further sound design to push it in the opposite direction: darker, distorted, and slightly unsettling — a processed mirror of the other voice, holding the dialogue together as two extremes of the same logic.
