Halfway
Performer / Composer
2023-2024
Realized in collaboration with Bruno Fabrizio Sorba and published by Musicando Edizioni Musicali in March 2024.
Halfway is a series of four journeys born from a series of improvisations by Bruno Fabrizio Sorba (electronics) and Eduard Tampu (saxophone). A series of personal conversations mixing reality and imagination in which the focus is on the human psyche. The uncertainty, the doubt, the realisation that we are constantly changing and perhaps will never know ourselves. When will this journey end?

The Sound Memory of the Ruff
Composition / Sound Desing / Foley
2023-2024
Realized for the video installation of Tiina Majabacka. Laikku's Studio, Tampere, 6 January 2024 - 11 February 2024. Galleria Huuto, Helsinki, 28 March 2024 - 21 April 2024.
“The Sound Memory of the Ruff” is a video focusing on the sensory experience related to the ruff, especially sounds. In this project the collaboration with producer has ended with the production of a sound piece in which music and foley are combined to form the sound of the ruff. Noise to Noise
Composition / Sound Desing
2023
Presented in the paper 'Deep Learing with Audio: An explorative Syllabus for Music Composition and Production', AIMC 2023 with Koray Tahiroğlu, Shenran Wang and Jackie Lin
The piece was realized at the end of the course Deep Learning with Audio at Aalto University and it is generated with only samples produced through AI. The piece explores the story of the lifecycle through the samples generated using GANSpaceSynth, from Noise to Noise.
