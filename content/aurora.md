*AURORA* is an interactive installation and Master's thesis project by Tino Selić (Aalto University, 2024–2026), exploring embodied interaction as a medium for children to express friendship. Using a ZED 2i stereo camera and Unreal Engine 5, the installation generates virtual aurora borealis that respond in real time to users' movements. The project was workshopped with 21 third-graders at the Helsinki German School in April 2024, inviting groups of children to collaborate through physical play and social interaction.

I contributed sound design for both the installation environment and the final documentation video. The audio was designed to complement the visual language of the piece — immersive and atmospheric, following the same logic as the auroras themselves: ambient, shifting, tied to movement and presence rather than imposing a fixed narrative.
