Born in Romania in 1998 and raised in Italy. Based in Helsinki since 2022, I'm studying in the MA Sound in New Media at Aalto University. I’m a Saxophonist (2020, BA degree with honours in Classical Saxophone from Conservatory of Music Cesare Pollini in Padua, Italy).

I developed my technical skills in Music Technology and Multimedia Arts independently by studying and practicing with software like Max/MSP, PureData, Ableton and TouchDesigner. To accomplish the artistic needs of my projects, I also developed soft programming skills.
